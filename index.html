About the Workshop

ICS-2026 is a two-day national workshop focusing on Intelligent Control Systems and their applications beyond traditional digital environments. The workshop brings together researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and students to explore advancements in control theory, artificial intelligence, smart systems, and Industry 4.0 technologies.
